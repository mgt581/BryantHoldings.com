Privacy Policy

This page explains how Bryant Group Holdings Ltd and related group entities handle privacy, responsibilities, and service boundaries across the group.

1. Company Identity

Bryant Group Holdings Ltd is a registered company in England and Wales. Companies House registration number: 13671131.

In this policy, references to "Bryant Group Holdings", "we", "us", or "our" refer to Bryant Group Holdings Ltd unless stated otherwise.

2. Group Companies and Trading Names

Under the Bryant Group Holdings group, we own and operate the following companies and brands:

  • Mr White Teeth Whitening Bournemouth
  • Bryant Construction Group (also known as Bryant Construct)
  • Bryant & Co Cleaning
  • Bryant Digital Solutions

These entities are underwritten and registered within the Bryant Group Holdings structure.

3. Separate Privacy Policies

Each company may collect, use, and store data differently depending on the services it provides. For that reason, each company has or may have its own privacy policy on its own website.

Please read the privacy policy of the specific company you are dealing with, as privacy terms may differ between separate entities.

4. Legal and Financial Advice Disclaimer

Bryant Group Holdings and its group companies do not provide legal or financial advice. Any information provided on our websites, in messages, or in marketing content is for general information only and should not be relied on as legal or financial guidance.

5. Insurance Position

All companies are insured separately under their own policies. Insurance held by each group company is separate from any policy arrangements held by Bryant Group Holdings Ltd.

6. Financial Management

Financial operations and oversight for group companies are managed by Bryant Group Holdings Ltd as part of its group management function.

7. Results and Outcomes

We are committed to delivering the services we advertise and working toward strong outcomes. However, results depend on circumstances and cannot be guaranteed in every case.

8. Contact

For privacy queries related to Bryant Group Holdings Ltd, please contact info@bryantholdings.com.
